Health2GPX Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026

Health2GPX ("the app") does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

Health Data

The app accesses Apple Health workout data only with your explicit permission in order to export them as GPX files.

All data is processed locally on your device.

Health data is never stored externally or transmitted to any server.

Data Collection

Health2GPX does not collect any personal information.

No analytics, tracking, or third-party services are used.

Data Sharing

Your data is never transmitted to external servers.

You are in full control of exporting and sharing your GPX files.

Permissions

The app requests access to Apple Health data solely for the purpose of reading workout data.
